3D Maze Go — Privacy Policy

Effective July 4, 2026

The short version

3D Maze Go does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics trackers, and no ads.

What stays on your device

Your level progress, difficulty preference, and local play statistics (such as misses per level) are stored only on your device and are never sent anywhere. Deleting the app deletes them.

Purchases

The optional "Unlock Infinite Levels" purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. Purchases can be restored through Apple at any time.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.
